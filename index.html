About Us

Mashek , A Non-Profit Organisation ,set up with the sole aim of reaching out to the poor and financially backward classes of society to help them in Gaining EDUCATION and we also work for the MALNUTRITION Hit children and reach out to them with 'DIETRY SUPPLEMENTS' and other necessities. Your single click on that DONATE NOW button may help someone in heading towards a better life and happiness, Maybe, later enrich our society, who knows! Allah bless. MASHEK with your donations help those children who wish to be a support to their families, who wish to be on the same height where you are, who simply wish to take home Bread and Butter when they complete their schooling. MASHEK paves a way forward for the schooling of such children by providing them with free books , notebooks , other stationary items, water bottles , lunch boxes , Hand Sanitizers, etc MASHEK also provides Free MEDICINES to the people who suffer everyday due to pain and agony, who are living on heights, snow clad mountains , remote villages of Kashmir. When You Donate Stationery Or Cash To MASHEK , We Contribute On Your Behalf , To All Those Children Living In Remote Villages Of Kashmir, Who Are In Dire Need Of 'Water, Medicines, Food Supplements And Stationery Items' To Live a Happy, Hygienic And Educated Life. It Would Help Change The Lives Of The Unprivileged Children.
Mashek,an initiative that stands firm on the belief of changing world for better. We belive “actions speak louder than words” and each act of humanity shall contribute gigantically towards a better future. Let’s stand together and help in laying the foundation of Educated Kashmir.

Success Stories

Distribution Events

Mashek with the help of its partner vertical amble mountanerring distributed medicines (food supplements), under the supervision of medical practitioners , worth thousands of rupees among nomads and other people living in remote villages of kashmir, like aru, pahalgam and tarsar-marsar.

KULGAM EVENT

Giving a glimpse of hope to all those unprivileged children , who feel the absence of money , will land them into a place where there is no place for dreams. Mashek in its event on( 25th nov , 2017 ) distributed free study material including notebooks , pens , pencils , erasers , sharpners , scales , school bags , etc among some poor and needy children , at " govt middle school , kounsarbal" ( zone dh pora) in district kulgam.

ANANTNAG EVENT

Mashek in its 2nd "collection event" held today (22nd june), at eid gagh islamabad received around 250 old books and some stationery items. Mostly the young children participated in the event. We couldn't give them anything in return but they all returned with a happy face and posed for a photograph with a smile.
